📖 How To Play

1. Pavilion & Market 🏪

Welcome to the Ultimate Cricket Manager! Your journey starts at the Pavilion.

  • Leaderboards: Track your World Points and NRR globally.
  • Transfer Market: Buy legendary players using Coins 🪙.
  • Premium Store: Get Coins and Diamonds 💎 to unlock premium features.
  • Tournaments: Play World Cup, Champ Cup (T10), or unlock the mega HANDS CUP at 100 points!

2. Squad Selection & Draft 👥

Building the right team is the key to winning championships.

  • Captain Selection: You will get 3 "Rarely" (Mega Star) player cards. Pick 1 as your Captain.
  • Auto-Fill: The game will automatically fill your squad to 19 players.
  • Playing 12: Before a match, you must select exactly 12 players from your squad.
  • Rule: Your Playing 12 MUST have at least 6 Batters and 5 Bowlers!

3. Match Gameplay 🏏

The game runs on the classic "Hand Cricket" logic. You and the AI select a number from 1 to 6.

BAT: 6 🆚 BOWL: 1
Result: 6 RUNS! 🎉
BAT: 2 🆚 BOWL: 2
Result: WICKET! 💀 (Numbers Matched)

If numbers match, it's an OUT. If they differ, the batsman scores the runs they selected.

4. Classic Test Match Masterclass 🔴

Test matches require patience and strategy across 5 Days and multiple Sessions.

  • Defense (0): Play "0" to Defend. It scores no runs but increases your Batsman's Confidence and saves stamina.
  • Stamina System: You cannot hit big shots if your batsman is tired!
    ➔ Need 75%+ Stamina to hit a 4.
    ➔ Need 86%+ Stamina to hit a 5.
    ➔ Need 99%+ Stamina to hit a SIX.
  • Declarations: If you have a massive lead, you can Declare your innings early to force a result!
